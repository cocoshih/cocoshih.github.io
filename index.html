About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the College of Public Health at George Mason University. My research focuses on the intersection of health informatics and public health, with a particular interest in data analytics and machine learning applications in healthcare.

I am passionate about using technology to improve health outcomes and enhance the efficiency of healthcare systems. I have experience in data analysis, programming, and research methodologies, and I am committed to advancing the field of health informatics through my work.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, reading, and exploring new technologies. I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow in my field, and I welcome any collaboration or networking opportunities.

Thank you for visiting my website! Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to connect.
